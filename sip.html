Project Title

AI-Driven Decentralized Digital Identity Wallet

Problem Statement

Individuals manage hundreds of digital accounts, leading to password fatigue, insecure credential reuse, and increased identity theft. Existing identity systems are centralized, outdated, and dependent on human memory.

Innovation Claim

This project introduces a decentralized digital identity wallet that merges blockchain-based identity ownership with AI-powered behavioral fraud detection and continuous authentication.

Project Description

User credentials are securely stored on a blockchain while machine learning models analyze behavioral patterns to detect fraud and authenticate users without relying on passwords.
