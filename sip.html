Project Title

AI-Driven Decentralized Digital Identity Wallet

Problem Statement

The increasing complexity of cyber attacks suggests there is also a need for a functional, intelligent, user-owned digital identity, which reduces mental load and offers a sustained, real-time form of security.

Innovation Claim

Innovation: An AI-powered decentralized digital identity system that merges identity ownership on blockchain with fraud detection based on machine learning. Innovative MVP: The new MVP will demonstrate a functioning decentralized identity wallet which will include an AI behavior risk model.

Project Description

This project will create an autonomous digital identity wallet that will store user credentials on the blockchain securely and use AI models to track behavior, identify potential fraud, and perform authentication with a zero-knowledge proof.
